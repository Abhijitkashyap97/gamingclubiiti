Gaming Club IITI

Our gaming club unites enthusiasts from all corners of the world to celebrate the passion for gaming. Whether you are looking to dive into casual play or take on the fiercest competitors, we offer a welcoming environment for every level of gamer. Join us to participate in thrilling tournaments, explore new game releases, and build lasting friendships. From strategy games to eSports competitions, we have something for everyone.

Valorant is a tactical, team-based FPS by Riot Games, combining precise shooting mechanics with unique character abilities.

Rocket League

Rocket League is a high-octane blend of soccer and rocket-powered cars, where players compete in fast-paced matches.

Fifa

FIFA is a popular football video game series by EA that allows players to simulate football matches and compete in various leagues worldwide.
